Max Temperatures

The latitude vs. temperature plot tells us that, the max. temps increase from the poles towards the Equator in both North and South Hemisphere. This is called "Latitude Effect" in Geography (By the way, the cities in Southern Hemisphere have negative latitude numbers while the Northerners have positive). However, temperatures make a peak at around 20 degrees North latitude, not exactly at the equator line. This can be explained due to the different cooling and warming features of oceans and lands.
